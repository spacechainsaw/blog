Stephany (she/her).

A Brazilian software engineer in Vancouver navigating the intersection of tech, mysticism, and philosophy. My work involves building digital systems, but my passion lies in dismantling the social ones. This blog is a repository for my deep dives into how technology rewrites our humanity and how we can rewrite ourselves. For over eight years, I have been deeply devoted to the slow, heavy work of internal integration and healing from the ground up. I believe we should spend less time protecting egos and more time questioning the algorithms, both digital and psychological, that we live by.
